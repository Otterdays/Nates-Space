Feed Music Platforms

Music & indie platforms

Curated links for posting tracks, reaching fans, and managing releases. Tap a row to open the site. This is a reference list — not an endorsement; check each platform’s terms and fees.

Host & sell (indie-first)

  • Bandcamp

    Direct-to-fan sales, merch, mailing list; strong indie culture.

    bandcamp.com

  • SoundCloud

    Uploads, embeds, comments; common for demos and electronic scenes.

    soundcloud.com

  • Audiomack

    Free streaming uploads; hip-hop and emerging artists.

    audiomack.com

  • Audius

    Decentralized-style streaming community for independent uploads.

    audius.co

  • BandLab

    Free DAW in the browser plus social publishing.

    bandlab.com

DIY distribution (DSPs)

  • DistroKid

    Flat annual fee; fast uploads to major streaming services.

    distrokid.com

  • TuneCore

    Pay-per-release or subscription; broad store coverage.

    tunecore.com

  • CD Baby

    One-time fee per release; distribution + optional publishing.

    cdbaby.com

  • Amuse

    Free tier available; mobile-first release flow.

    amuse.io

  • UnitedMasters

    Distribution with brand sync opportunities.

    unitedmasters.com

  • RouteNote

    Free and premium tiers; wide DSP reach.

    routenote.com

  • Ditto Music

    Independent distributor with recurring plans.

    dittomusic.com

  • LANDR

    Mastering tools plus distribution in one ecosystem.

    landr.com

  • SoundOn

    TikTok-affiliated distribution and promo tools.

    soundon.global

Artist dashboards (DSPs)

  • Spotify for Artists

    Pitch releases, see stats, manage profile after your music is live.

    artists.spotify.com

  • Apple Music for Artists

    Apple Music / iTunes insights and profile tools.

    artists.apple.com

  • YouTube for Artists

    Official artist channel and video music ecosystem.

    youtube.com/artists

  • Amazon Music for Artists

    Streaming and Alexa reach for Amazon Music.

    artists.amazon.com

  • Deezer Backstage for Artists

    Analytics and profile for Deezer.

    backstage.deezer.com

  • Pandora AMP

    Artist marketing tools on Pandora (where available).

    amp.pandora.com

Social & video

  • TikTok

    Short video; huge music discovery via sounds and trends.

    tiktok.com

  • Instagram

    Reels, Stories, link in bio for drops and tour dates.

    instagram.com

  • YouTube

    Official videos, Shorts, livestreams, long-form content.

    youtube.com

Discovery & promo

  • SubmitHub

    Submit tracks to bloggers, playlisters, and labels (credits-based).

    submithub.com

  • Groover

    Guaranteed feedback from curators and media (paid).

    groover.co

  • Hype Machine

    Aggregates music blogs; discovery for indie tracks.

    hypem.com

  • ReverbNation

    Artist tools, opportunities, and promotion (long-running indie hub).

    reverbnation.com

Beats & licensing

  • BeatStars

    Sell beats and licenses to vocalists and brands.

    beatstars.com

  • Traktrain

    Invite-based beat marketplace; common in producer circles.

    traktrain.com

Tools & community

  • Splice

    Samples, rent-to-own plugins, project backup.

    splice.com

  • Genius

    Lyrics, annotations, artist verification.

    genius.com

  • r/WeAreTheMusicMakers

    Large Reddit community for production and career questions.

    reddit.com/r/WeAreTheMusicMakers

  • Patreon

    Membership tiers for fans — stems, early drops, live Q&As.

    patreon.com

  • Ko-fi

    Tips and simple memberships without heavy setup.

    ko-fi.com
